About Us

Safaricom is the leading telecommunication company in Kenya. We are a purpose-led technology company that is driven by our passion for our customers, our communities and our planet. Our mission is to transform lives by connecting people to people, people to knowledge, and people to opportunities.

We provide a wide range of products and services for individuals and enterprises. Our offerings include mobile voice and data, M-PESA, financial services, and enterprise solutions. We are committed to innovation and excellence, striving to deliver world-class services that enrich the lives of Kenyans.
